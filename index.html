Governance & methodology

Theoretical foundation & operating principles

The registry complements — does not replace — LFPSE, MHRA Yellow Card, and trust-level incident reporting. Classification grounded in the MIT AI Risk Repository.

— Theoretical foundation
MIT AI Risk Repository
A comprehensive living database of over 1,700 AI risks, with a Causal Taxonomy (Entity · Intent · Timing) and a Domain Taxonomy (7 domains, 24 subdomains). Every incident in this registry is classified against both taxonomies, giving NHS clinical safety officers a peer-reviewed shared vocabulary to use when authoring DCB0129 hazard analyses.
